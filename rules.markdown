# The Rules

1. If you're not having fun, you're doing something wrong.  
2. Throw things up and back, not at the screen or the cast.

4. Do not touch the cast. Costumes do not equal consent.  
5. If you are asked to move, sit down, or get out of the aisle, please do so.  
6. No getting pregnant in line.  
7. If you don't participate, you can't come.
