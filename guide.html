Audience Participation Guide

So you're going to see The Rocky Horror Picture Show for the first time, and you're not sure what to expect. Or maybe you've seen it before, but you're not sure what you're supposed to do. Or maybe you're just looking for a refresher. Well, you've come to the right place!

Your Attention Please

  1. The Rocky Horror Picture Show is rated R, and it contains material that some people may find offensive.
  2. The cult classic audience participation experience may encourage individuals within the audience to say and do things that some people may find offensive.
  3. Performers may be moving throughout the theater in various states of undress during the performance, and at times may interact physically, spiritually, and psychically with the audience.
