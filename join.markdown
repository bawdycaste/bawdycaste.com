There aren't many formal requirements â€” for the most part, you can decide how involved you want to be.

<div class="glass-panel p-4 rounded my-4 border-start border-4 border-danger">
    <p><strong>Staying in Touch</strong><br>
    Keep in touch via Discord, texts, or phone calls. If you'll be unavailable for a while, let the directors know.</p>

    <p><strong>Checking In & Attending Shows</strong><br>
    Respond to check-in reminders about upcoming shows. Directors will announce casting about a week before each show.</p>

    <p><strong>Cast Meetings</strong><br>
    We meet four times a year (February, May, August, November) to discuss big decisions. You're required to attend at least one meeting per year, but encouraged to attend all!</p>

    <p><strong>Dues</strong><br>
    $5 per quarter, collected once per year. If you can't afford it, let the Financial Secretary know and dues can be waived.</p>

## ğŸ‘‘ Cast Leadership

Most decisions are made democratically, but we elect people to specific roles:

<div class="glass-panel p-4 rounded my-4 border-danger border-opacity-25 border">
    <p><strong>Cast Directors</strong> (Elected every 2 years)<br>
    Point of contact ensuring departments work smoothly. They help identify issues and get people together to solve them.</p>

    <p><strong>Financial Secretary</strong> (Elected every 2 years)<br>
    Tracks cast funds and reports to the cast. Helps budget for cool projects.</p>

    <p><strong>Cast Council</strong><br>
    Directors, Financial Secretary, and department heads. Decides on urgent matters between cast meetings.</p>
</div>

## âš ï¸ General Guidelines

<div class="alert alert-danger border-danger border-2 text-white bg-danger bg-opacity-10">
    <strong class="text-danger fs-5">Sexual Harassment: Don't do it!</strong><br>
    We take this extremely seriously. Yes, people are scantily clad and sex jokes are the norm, but this must feel safe for all genders, sexualities, and orientations. <strong>Consent in all things</strong> is our highest value. If uncomfortable, bring it to leadership â€” you will be taken seriously.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-warning border-warning border-2 text-white bg-warning bg-opacity-10">
    <strong class="text-warning fs-5">Discrimination is Not Tolerated</strong><br>
    Offensive jokes are part of our AP lines, but intentionally cruel remarks about sex, race, religion, sexual preference, gender identity, etc. are not acceptable. <strong>Be offensive, be shocking, but pay attention to whether you're punching up or down.</strong> The foundation of our cast is that everyone is accepted and worthy of respect.
</div>

<div class="alert alert-info border-info border-2 text-white bg-info bg-opacity-10">
    <strong class="text-info fs-5">Priorities Matter</strong><br>
    Your health, family, school, work, and other commitments are more important than Rocky. It's perfectly acceptable to check out for personal obligations. <strong>Rocky Horror is not 100% of anyone's life.</strong> Just give as much notice as you can when checking out of a show.
</div>
